The Multi-Template Ladder (or MTL) is a 1v1 ladder similar to the other ladders on Warzone. Unlike the 1v1 ladder on Warzone, this ladder utilizes {{templates|length}} of Warzone's most popular strategic templates.


Once you join the ladder you will be able to veto up to 7 templates that you would prefer not to play games on.


All games honor vacations and have a 3 day auto-boot. It checks results and allocates new games every two hours.
